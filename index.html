Memes of the Decade

By Davis Bryant

Memes have exploded in popularity this Decade

The oxford dictionary defines meme as an element of a culture or system of behavior that may be considered to be passed from one individual to another by nongenetic means, especially imitation. The mainsteam adaptation of the internet in the 2000's created a perfect place for memes to spread, but memes did not gain mainstream appeal until the early 2010's, creating an entirely new way for people to communicate ideas. The purpose of this website is to analyze the transformation memes have experienced throughout this decade, and to determine which is the best.
