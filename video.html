Video is also essential to meme culture

Video took a little longer to become mainstream meme culture, but has arguably become the most popular way to experience memes. The first mainstream meme on youtube were youtube poops, which were crucial for making youtube the new place to look for memes. Later on, websites like vine and instagram made video memes much shorter, and this is the most common form they exist in today. I choose We are number one, because it started the trend of replacinga specific word in a music video with something else, which dominated youtube for a few years.
